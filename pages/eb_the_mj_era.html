    • Who Was Michael Jordan?

    Michael Jeffrey Jordan is heavily regarded as one of, if not, the greatest basketball player of all time. Standing at 6 feet and 6 inches, he was well known for having an outstanding defensive game, and holding a point average of 30.1 points per game. Jordan won 6 championships throughout his whole career, was a 14-time NBA All-Star, and played for the Chicago Bulls, as well as the Washington Wizards after a short-lived retirement. He became one of the main reasons the NBA blew up in popularity around the 1980's, and his legacy molded him into an icon for basketball all around the world.

    The Impact of Michael Jordan's Legacy

    Michael Jordan's popularity led him into appearing in lots of media as well as brand deals. One such example being Nike's Air Jordans, a brand of sneakers which became and remain incredibley popular to this day. As for media, Jordan was featured in 1996's Space Jam as the starring role, and a documentary mini-series called The Last Dance. He also made appearances in a plethora of advertisments and commercials. He was even able to start his own clothing line and a motorsports racing team after his final retirement.

Rivalry in the Making

With how bad the NBA's reputation was from all the drug abuse and violence, the league needed a miracle in order to stay in business. That miracle turned out to be Larry Bird and Magic Johnson. Long before the NBA, Bird and Johnson already had quite the rivalry throughout their college basketball years. In fact, it was quite well known too. A game between Indiana and Michigan (which each of them played for respectively) was the most watched college basketball game of all time. Their rivalry would continue in the NBA after joining around the same time. Bird in Celtics, and Johnson in Lakers. Viewership shifted to an all-time high, and these two saved the NBA as we know it.
